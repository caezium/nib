# Quality bar

Reject and regenerate if any fail:

- [ ] **White background**, generous empty space — not a busy or colored scene.
- [ ] **One idea only** — not a multi-panel explainer crammed into one frame
      (unless a mini-comic was intended).
- [ ] **The avatar is performing the action** and is clearly the subject —
      not standing in a corner as decoration.
- [ ] **The avatar is recognizable** and consistent with the reference
      (shape, colors, proportions).
- [ ] **Labels are short** (1–4 words) and few, spelled correctly, away from
      the avatar's face. No title; no structure name written on the image.
- [ ] **Reads at article width** — the metaphor is legible as a thumbnail.
- [ ] **Not** a photo, logo, corporate infographic, formal flowchart, or UI.

If the model misspells a long label, drop the label and regenerate. If the look
drifted, restate the style and regenerate rather than editing.
