What does it cost?

Free with a Codex or ChatGPT plan

The skill itself is free and open source — and whatever agent you run it in, the image generation goes through one of two backends. With a Codex / ChatGPT plan it's free: Nib drives your signed-in Codex CLI to render the image on your existing subscription, no per-image charge — it just draws on your Codex usage.

Want a choice of models instead? Run it through your own OpenRouter account — pay-per-image, around a nickel each (6–25¢ by model), a full blog post under $0.50.

Runs inside the agent you already use.
